Privacy Policy for Tusk

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Voice Data: We collect the audio recordings you provide to transcribe and categorize your tasks.
  • Task Data: We store the text transcripts, categories (Work, Medical, Food, General), and reminder times you save.
  • Account Data: Your email and name if you create an account.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core functionality of voice-to-task conversion.
  • To categorize your tasks using Artificial Intelligence.
  • To send you notifications for your scheduled reminders.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

Tusk uses third-party AI services (such as OpenAI or Google Gemini) to process voice-to-text. Your audio is sent to these providers for transcription but is not used by them to train their global models (verify this based on the API you use).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and voice recordings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, edit, or delete your data at any time via the Settings menu in the Tusk app.
