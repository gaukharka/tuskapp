Privacy Policy for Tusk

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Voice Data: We collect the audio recordings you provide to transcribe and categorize your tasks.
  • Task Data: We store the text transcripts, categories (Work, Medical, Shopping, Family, General, Personal), reminder times, and completion status of your tasks and ideas
  • Account Data: Your email and name if you create an account.
  • App Preferences: We store your app preferences locally on your device, including reminder settings, theme preferences (dark/light mode), interface language, and voice transcription language settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core functionality of voice-to-task conversion and categorization.
  • To categorize your tasks and ideas using using Artificial Intelligence.
  • To send you local push notifications for your scheduled task reminders.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

  • OpenAI: Tusk uses third-party AI services (such as OpenAI or Google Gemini) to process voice-to-text. Your audio is sent to these providers for transcription but is not used by them to train their global models. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's privacy policy for more details.

  • Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) We use Firebase for user authentication and cloud storage of your task data. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your task data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with encryption in transit and at rest. App preferences are stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and voice recordings.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained until you delete your account or individual tasks. You can delete your account at any time via the Settings menu in the Tusk app, which will permanently delete all your stored data.

6. Your Rights

- Access your data at any time within the app

- Delete your tasks and ideas

- Delete your entire account and all associated data via the Settings menu

7. Children's Privacy

Tusk is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
